Ingredients:
 - 170g all-purpose flour
 - 80g butter
 - 30g sugar
 - 30g brown sugar
 - 1 egg
 - 1/4 tsp salt
 - 100g chocolate chips

Directions:
 1. blend the butter until creamy
 2. add salt and sugar
 3. add egg
 4. filter the flour and add them into wet ingredients.
 5. add chocolate chips.
 6. put them onto baking plate
 7. bake 15 minutes in oven with 170 degree
